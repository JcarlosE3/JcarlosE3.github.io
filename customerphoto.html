Customer Flooring

These are some of the pictures I took from customers that I have work with in the past. Each of these customers were amazed on how our floor looked and I even took pride of my work. I love my job because it gives me the chance to make people happy my making there homes feel more like home with some cool flooring. I started this job at the beggining of summer in 2020. Ever since then I've had the joy of learning new things and becoming better each day. There is still more to learn and I cant wait to get back out there and learn as much as I can. At the end of the day, it is about the customer and to make sure they love their floors! Enjoy the photos.
