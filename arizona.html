Nevada Trip

This trip to Nevada and Arizona was one for the books and I had such an amazing time exploring and seeing what is out there in the world. Of course we had to go to the infamous Las Vegas. It was so much fun and there are some crazy things that happen in Vegas. My football team, The Las Vegas Raiders, Just moved there and that is now there hometown. I cant wait to go to one of there games. It should be really great. There is much more than Arizona in my opinion. There are a few stops that we took and It was honestly really fun going to these places. One place we went to was this art piece that is in the middle of the desert! Nobody knows how it got there but the artist did come out and say it was theirs. It was one of the craziest things I've seen. Along the trip, in Arizona they also have some cool mountains that I've never seen in Fresno. It was Awesome!
