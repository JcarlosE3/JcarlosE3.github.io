I love basketball

The game of Basketball

First of all, learning basketball by myself was tough. Its such an honest sport and is very simple. I enjoy wathching basketball, I enjoy talking about basketball, and I love to play basketball. Its one of the many things that I love to do and I will probably never get tired of it. At first it started off as just a thing I did in middle school to it being my one of many sources of happiness. I want to learn more about basketball and I want to make sure I end up doing something in life that involves basketball. I dont know how much times I have said 'basketball' already! But that goes to show on how much I love it! A quick story, I remember trying out for the team in middle school and I had got cut. It tore me to pieces because I really wanted to play. That day I got cut I worked on every single thing until I perfected it. Now whatever I do, if i fail, I go back and keep doing that certain thing that I failed and perfect it.

Basketball Legends I look up to

Now there is always a debate on who the greatest basketball player out there is. I personally think every players has a unique type of play style. But it will always come up on who I believe is the greatest to ever play the game. Before I would always say Lebron James. He does many great things off the court and thats why I admire him. He is the king of basektball today and will forever go down as the greatest of all time. Now, ive changed my mind and now believe Michael Jordan is the greatest. Not only does he have 6 championship rings, but he has never lost a finals game. He was one of the first basetkball players to wear his own shoes and has changed the game from what it was. He made it look so easy, and I will forever look up to him. Another great basketball player is the late Kobe Bryant. I was sad when I saw that he had passed away. He was one of the reasons I started playing basektball. He is the legend of basketball and you cant talk about basketball without ever mentioning his name. R.I.P to the legend Kobe Bryant no. 8/24
