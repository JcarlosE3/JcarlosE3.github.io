Paragraph about photography

When I first got into photography, I thought it was hard. I thought you had to have the best camera to get the best photo. But that is not the case, from what I learned It always about the person taking the photo. The person that creates the art and the meaning behind the photos. Telling the stories of photos and making it mean something to everybody who is viewing it. Below are some of my photos that I believe represent this. I hope to one day get my photos to get recognized and brought up. I want to continue this passion because its most important to keep memories forever.
