Paragraph about photography

First of all, learning basketball by myself was tough. Its such an honest sport and is very simple. I enjoy wathching basketball, I enjoy talking about basketball, and I love to play basketball. Its one of the many things that I love to do and I will probably never get tired of it. At first it started off as just a thing I did in middle school to it being my one of many sources of happiness. I want to learn more about basketball and I want to make sure I end up doing something in life that involves basketball. I dont know how much times I have said 'basketball' already! But that goes to show on how much I love it! A quick story, I remember trying out for the team in middle school and I had got cut. It tore me to pieces because I really wanted to play. That day I got cut I worked on every single thing until I perfected it. Now whatever I do, if i fail, I go back and keep doing that certain thing that I failed and perfect it.
